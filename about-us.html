About us


Arabic book fair in Malmö is the first of its kind in Sweden and Scandinsvia. The Swedish city of Malmö hosts the permenent headquarters of the Arab Book Fair in Scandinavia and Sweden, Where the launch of the headquarters came in earlt November 2021 to be a house for Arab books in Europ.

Regarding the choice of the city of Malmö to be the permenent location for the exhibition stems from the importance of the social and cultural diversity that the city forms, in addition to the fact that the Arabic language is the second language in the city after the Swedish language, on one hand. On the other hand the importance of the Arabic book and freedom of though by achieving positiv integration by Arabic speakers in their societies, especially with regard to issues of womwn's and children's rights and respect for others.

It is worth noting that the session of the Arab Book Fair in Europ, which held from 20 - 25 August, 2021, had achieved more than 412 thousand visitors.
